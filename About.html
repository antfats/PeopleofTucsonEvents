About Us

Welcome to People of Tucson Events page. The focus of our page is for local Tucsonans to share what’s going on around our community. Tucson is a historical place full of never ending happenings. The beautiful weather brings people from around the county to share in Tucson’s cultural spender. From Mt. Lemons’ pine trees to Tucson’s historical downtown nightlife, you will be sure to find events that will keep you busy from dusk til dawn.

Share your events with us whether you are hosting or attending. We intend to keep the public up to date on all things Tucson. Surrounding Tucson Events are also welcome. We are close to fun places to visit including Tombstone, Bisbee, Phoenix, Tubac, and more. Events can be anything from great hiking trails to bands coming to town. We would appreciate your input to keep others apprised to interesting things going on around Tucson. Know of a place that is promoting a special others would like to know about? Share it!

The creators of People Of Tucson Events are Anthony Fatato, Jadon Rodriguez, Ryan Grady, and Nicolle Chamberlain. They are part of University of Arizona’s Coding Bootcamp continuing education program. When asked to create something super cool the People of Tucson Events page was born. Share our “something super cool” with us by sharing your Events.
